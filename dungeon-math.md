# Dungeon Math

## XP Rules
- **Treasure XP**: 1 GP = 1 XP
- **Magic Items**: 
  - +1 weapons/armor/shields = 400 XP
  - +2 weapons/armor/shields = 3,200 XP  (not available until level 4)
  - +3 weapons/armor/shields = 24,000 XP (not available until level 7)
- **Spells & Scrolls** (XP value by spell level):
  - Level 1: 100 XP
  - Level 2: 375 XP     (not available until level 3)
  - Level 3: 1,000 XP   (not available until level 5)
  - Level 4: 3,000 XP   (not available until level 7)
  - Level 5: 6,250 XP   (not available until level 9)
  - Level 6: 15,000 XP  (not available until level 11)

## Encounters per Level
Every 3 level advancements requires one additional encounter:
- **Levels 2-4**: 13 encounters
- **Levels 5-7**: 14 encounters
- **Levels 8-10**: 15 encounters
- **Levels 11-14**: 16 encounters

## Encounter Distribution
Consistent distribution across all levels:
- **Easy**: 60%
- **Moderate**: 30%
- **Hard**: 10%

## Treasure Distribution Rules
- **Coins**: 10% of total XP
- **Non-magical valuables** (gems, artifacts, books, art): 40% of total XP
- **Consumable magic** (scrolls, potions): 20% of total XP
- **Permanent magic items** (weapons, armor, shields): 30% of total XP
